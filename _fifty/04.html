--- number: 4 layout: fifty title: Psychadelic Tunnel description: Exaggerating the cartoonish aesthetic of Glow 3, this glow effect begins to enter the realm of psychadelia, entirely leaving behind any kind of authentic glowing effect. Instead of placing a single, white circle behind the object, this effect is implemented by nesting hundreds of rainbow-colored circles within each other. When the object is clicked, all of of these circles expand, creating a psychadelic, tunnel effect.
