--- number: 11 layout: fifty title: Exploding Star description: Thinking about other shapes that indicate glowing, I thought about stars. Creating a star, it turns out, is no small task in HTML and CSS. In this effect, I wanted to place a star behind the object and spin and grow it until the entire background was filled. The star, created by drawing a triangle using the border attributes and then using an :after pseudo-selector, then proved hard control and move around. The effect I wound up with is a star that spins out from behind the object, growing and moving back to center in a kind of lasso-like effect. permalink: fifty/11/ ---
