--- number: 15 layout: fifty title: A Field Of Neon Lights In A Mist description: But the field of neon lights in Glow 14 is still so geometrical. It was a nice effect with a nice origin story, but it didn't do what I hoped it would. In this next glowing field, I revised my first draft to capture the softness of the edges of the light given off by neon. What I love about neon lights is that, no matter the weather, they seem to be in mist. The glow they give off seems almost viscous. Between the movement and shapes and soft edges, I often can't help but imagine neon light is reflecting off of smoke or fog. This glow effect returns to the box-shadow effect from glows of yore. It attempts to straddle the natural and the man-made, drawing a line between neon and moonlight, mist and smog, trying to capture that almost wet property in the glow of neon. permalink: fifty/15/ ---
