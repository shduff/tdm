In this glowing field, I tried to capture the softness of the edges of the light given off by neon. Between the movement and shapes and soft edges, I couldn't help but imagine the effect of light reflecting off of smoke or fog. This glow effect straddles the natural and the man-made, drawing a line between neon and moonlight, mist and smog. permalink: /15/ ---
