--- number: 21 layout: fifty title: Glowing Butts description: In a riff off the single firefly in Glow 20, I wanted to create the effect of a swarmof fireflies around the object. This was mostly an implementation change, in which I moved the firefly creation into the realm of Javascript, adjusting their size and quantity, adding an animation to make them buzzor shake rather than hover and glide in perfect lines, assigning random z-indexes (so some would fly in front and other behind the object), starting points above the screen (so they didn't all originate in a single spot), and intervals at which they move from point to point (so they weren't moving in synchrony). The illusion of a glowing swarm! permalink: fifty/21/ ---
