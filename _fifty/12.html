--- number: 12 layout: fifty title: Walk Of Fame description: In a variation of Glow 11 and inspired by the movement I'd created while unable to get the star to move how I'd hoped, I wanted to use a star in a different kind of glow effect. The first star effect was based on the idea that a star, quite literally, glows. But stars also have symbolic meanings connected with metaphorical glows. For this effect, I created a five-pointed (much more involved than a six-pointed!) star and reused the not-quite-what-I'd-wanted movement from the previous effect to create a Hollywood Walk of Fame style star. In this effect, the glow is less literal and more about the glow of recognition, fame, legacy. In this effect, the out-and-back movement of the star feels right. It's a gold star review. It's making an entrance. It's marking the object's success. Go get it, ecstatic object! permalink: fifty/12/ ---
