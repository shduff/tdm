--- number: 16 layout: fifty title: Homage To James Turrell description: With contemporary art on the brain after Glows 14 and 15, I was reminded of a series of light installations I saw at Mass MOCA, created by James Turrell. One in particular, called Into the Light, was an installation in which Turrell creates a perfectly white room with no hard edges. You had to wear little booties to avoid scuffing the floors. There were no corners, floor melting into wall. He sets up a series of projectors which cast light about in such a way that the room itself seems to glow, color melting into color, your sense of space and direction melting into eachother as time passes. In this effect, I used a keyframe animation to change the colors of a linear-gradient background to recreate the glowing, color-changing effect of Into The Light. permalink: fifty/16/ ---
