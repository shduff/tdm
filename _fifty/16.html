--- number: 16 layout: fifty title: Homage To James Turrell description: In perusing neon lights as a medium in contemporary art, I was reminded of [Into the Light](https://massmoca.org/event/james-turrell/), a James Turrell installation I saw at Mass MOCA. In it, Turrell creates a room with no edges, casting lights in such a way that the room itself seems to glow, color melting into color, your sense of space and direction getting rewired as time goes on. In this piece, I tried to create that sense, of being in a room that is being lit such that it seems to be glowing itself, with edges unclear, and light shining the shadows out. permalink: fifty/16/ ---
