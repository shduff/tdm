--- number: 24 layout: fifty title: Helion description: Inspired by the "natural" glow of Spencer Finch's installation from Glow 23, I found myself considering other natural light sources. A few years back, I worked with a student team working to design an installation that would give people the full sensory experience of being on a beach at sunrise. This idea was based on one team member's experience in her hometown in Ecuador, a place she was not able to return to in real life but often went in her imagination. The light there, she said, had a particular quality. This idea transformed into the idea of a lamp to which you could upload a photograph. From the photo, it would output a lighting effect which mirrored the color and tone of the picture it was fed. The glow of this sunrise is an homage to that student project, called Helion, and was implemented by using a keyframe animation to control the color change of a linear-gradient set as the page's background image. permalink: fifty/24/ ---
